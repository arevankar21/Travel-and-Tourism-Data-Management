Top destinations for India city trips

Goa

Goa, situated on India's western coast, boasts stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and rich history. Despite its size, it offers diverse experiences: water sports at Calangute, Baga, Palolem, and a fusion of Indian and Portuguese cuisine, highlighting dishes like fish curry and vindaloo.

Kashmir

Kashmir, often dubbed "Paradise on Earth," showcases snow-capped mountains, pristine valleys, and serene lakes in the Himalayas. Key attractions include Dal Lake, renowned for its iconic houseboats, and Gulmarg, famous for its skiing slopes and cable car rides.

Kerala

Kerala, "God's Own Country," captivates with lush greenery, tranquil backwaters, and serene beaches. Explore Alleppey's backwaters on a houseboat and Munnar's tea plantations for breathtaking views and memorable wildlife spotting adventures.

Coorg

Coorg, or Kodagu, nestled in Karnataka's Western Ghats, is famed for misty hills, coffee estates, and waterfalls. Abbey Falls, a serene waterfall, and Namdroling Monastery, a grand Tibetan Buddhist site, beckon nature enthusiasts.

Delhi

Delhi, India's vibrant capital, melds history with modernity, featuring iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar. The Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, showcases magnificent Mughal architecture.

Mumbai

Mumbai, India's bustling financial hub, embodies diversity and energy. Its iconic Gateway of India overlooks the Arabian Sea, while Marine Drive offers scenic views. Explore Bollywood with tours, and visit Elephanta Caves for rich cultural heritage.
